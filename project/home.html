About the Course

This course is designed to take you from a beginner to a professional graphic designer. You will learn the fundamentals of design, color theory, typography, and how to use industry-standard software.

By the end of this course, you will have a portfolio of work that showcases your skills and creativity.

Join us and start your journey in the world of graphic design!

Curriculum

  • Introduction to Graphic Design
  • Color Theory
  • Typography
  • Adobe Photoshop Basics
  • Adobe Illustrator Basics
  • Creating a Portfolio

Each module includes video lectures, quizzes, and hands-on projects to help you apply what you've learned.

By the end of the curriculum, you will have a comprehensive understanding of graphic design principles.
